About Us

With so many coffee shops to choose from how do you find the right one?

We decided to create this awesome website because we love independent coffee shops and think they are worth supporting.
Durham offers such a diverse range of cafes. Coffee Cat has them all in one place to help you pick the right one for your cafe needs. You want cake; we got cake. You want artisanal coffee; we got some of that too. You need a peaceful place to study; we know where they hide all the plugs and fast Wi-Fi.

Small wonder cats get the cream.
